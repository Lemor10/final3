Account Issues

If you are having trouble accessing your account, try the following:

  • Make sure your email address and password are correct.
  • Check your email for the verification link sent during registration.
  • If your account is not verified, click the verification link again.
  • If you forgot your password, use the password reset feature.
Dog Registration

To register your dog in the system:

  • Log in to your {{ brand_name }} account.
  • Go to the Dog Registration section.
  • Enter the dog’s information such as breed, age, and owner details.
  • Upload any required documents if necessary.
  • Submit the form to complete the registration.
Vaccination Tracking

The system helps monitor dog vaccination records:

  • Vaccination details can be recorded by authorized administrators.
  • Users can view vaccination history through their dashboard.
  • The system may notify users when vaccinations are due.
  • QR codes may be used to quickly access dog information.
